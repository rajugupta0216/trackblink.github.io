Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common queries about tracking, food orders, and tickets.

How accurate is the live train tracking?

Our tracking system combines official railway data with real-time GPS crowdsourcing from users on the train. This ensures highly accurate location updates and delay predictions, often faster than station displays.

What happens if my food order isn't delivered?

We track train delays to adjust delivery timings. However, in the rare case that a vendor misses your train, we process an automatic, full refund to your original payment method immediately.

Are digital platform tickets valid?

Yes, digital platform tickets booked via our app are fully authorized. You can simply show the active ticket screen or QR code to the Ticket Examiner (TTE) or security staff at the station entry.
